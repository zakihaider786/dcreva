What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device to help us provide a seamless and personalized experience on our website. At DCreva, we use cookies responsibly to respect your privacy.

How to Manage Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. While disabling cookies may affect your browsing experience, it ensures you have full control over your privacy.

  • Go to your browser settings.
  • Navigate to privacy or cookies section.
  • Adjust cookie preferences as needed.
